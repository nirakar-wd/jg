Delivery Time frame

Once your order is verified, we will process, pack, and schedule your package for delivery. We typically deliver your items within 48 hours inside Kathmandu valley (Kathmandu, Lalitpur, Bhaktapur).

Delivery is anticipated to take 1-2 working days in Kathmandu, Bhaktapur, and Lalitpur. We will need two working days after placing your purchase in order to process, schedule, and deliver it.
